Getting There
If you choose to fly into Indiana for the wedding the closest airport is Indianapolis International Airport, which is about one hour north of Bloomington. To get to Bloomington from the airport you can either rent a car OR take a shuttle. The hotels listed below are on a shuttle route. Flying in but don’t want to stay at either of the hotels listed below? Take the shuttle to the Indiana Memorial Union and grab an Uber or taxi from there.
